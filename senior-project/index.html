Project Description

My Senior Project in Unity 3D.

The purpose of the project was to demonstrate proficiency with Unity, connect Unity to Twitch via the TwitchLib library, and allow classmates to influence the build in real time.

The build was presented live in class in December 2022.

Development spanned approximately 3-4 months.

3D Environment

The entire 3D environment was designed, modeled, textured, and animated by me in Blender and imported into Unity.

My goal was to give the low-poly models and overall render a late 90's video game nostalgic feel.
